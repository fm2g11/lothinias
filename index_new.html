About the Author


Francesco Moramarco was born in Southern Italy, in a small village by the seaside, where he lived for the first 19 years of his life.

He studied Computer Science at the University of Southampton (UK) and Artificial Intelligence at NUS in Singapore. He currently works in Natural Language Processing in London.

He started writing Fantasy at the age of 14, driven by the works of Tolkien, Rowling and Paolini. The continent of 'Lothinias' was born, but would not see the light until 2018, with his debut novel 'Brothers of Fire'.

Aside from writing, he enjoys dancing salsa, bachata, kizomba and zouk.

Delwin has lived his whole life in the mountains, exiled for his father's crime of throwing the Holy Medallion into an active volcano, instead of returning it to the Silver City. Everything changes when he meets half-elf Aryanil, and the two realise the medallion may not have been destroyed in the volcanic eruption after all. If only they could find it, Delwin could redeem his family from exile, while Aryanil could harness its powers to become a wizard.
